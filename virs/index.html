SIMPLIFY

User-friendly interface and graphical outputs generate infectious disease models accessible to a wider audience unfamiliar with epidemiological modeling.

VISUALIZE

Integrating computer programming with geographic, social, and infrastructural datasets visually simulate the spread of EVD within affected communities.

PREDICT

Validated models more rapidly predict EVD and measure the impact of individual ETUs during the course of the outbreak.

RESPOND

Optimized models improve ground coordination and inform responders of ideal ETU placements to ensure early intervention to reduce case count and case mortality.
